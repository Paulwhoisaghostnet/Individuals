# Safety and accessibility

Requirements governing safe installation, public presentation, servicing, and
equitable visitor access. Final decisions must be made with the venue and qualified
local professionals.

Document and validate:

- accessible routes, viewing positions, reach ranges, and control-free experience;
- circulation capacity, queueing, trip hazards, protrusions, and low-light risks;
- emergency egress, alarm, sprinkler, fire-load, and evacuation constraints;
- structural, electrical, overhead, and equipment-temperature risks;
- installation and maintenance procedures including required protective equipment;
- privacy notice and proof that cameras are constrained to peer canvases.
