# Lighting

Ambient and controlled light affecting visitor experience, display visibility, and
camera perception.

Document and validate:

- daylight variation and venue lighting states across operating hours;
- illuminance and color temperature at each canvas and camera position;
- reflection, glare, flicker, shadow, flare, and light-spill risks;
- blackout, hood, flag, or dedicated illumination requirements;
- interaction with projection or emissive display technologies;
- repeatable lighting cues and venue control responsibilities.

Lighting changes that form part of an Individual's perception should be authored;
uncontrolled venue variation should be measured and bounded.
