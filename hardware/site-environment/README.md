# Site environment requirements

Venue conditions directly affect what cameras perceive, what canvases communicate,
and how safely visitors encounter the work. Each location requires a recorded site
survey before hardware is finalized.

Initial requirements:

- **ENV-001:** Ambient and controlled lighting must be documented at representative
  hours of operation.
- **ENV-002:** Temperature, humidity, dust, ventilation, and acoustic constraints
  must remain within equipment and venue limits.
- **ENV-003:** Visitor circulation, sight lines, reach zones, and accessible routes
  must be reviewed with the venue.
- **ENV-004:** Emergency exits, alarms, sprinklers, signage, and venue operations
  must not be obstructed.
- **ENV-005:** Camera placement must preserve the stated privacy boundary.
- **ENV-006:** Cleaning and routine venue activity must not disturb alignment or
  expose unsafe components.

See `lighting/` and `safety-accessibility/`.
