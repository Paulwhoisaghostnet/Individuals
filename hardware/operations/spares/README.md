# Spares and service stock

Replacement components, tools, configuration backups, and restoration procedures
needed to meet the installation's recovery targets.

Document and validate:

- criticality and acceptable downtime for every component class;
- quantity, storage location, shelf life, battery care, and periodic test schedule;
- preconfiguration and firmware compatibility for replacement devices;
- calibration transfer or recalibration requirements;
- end-of-life, substitution, and vendor discontinuation plan;
- failed-part labeling, data handling, warranty, and disposal procedure.
