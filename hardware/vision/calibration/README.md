# Vision calibration

Procedures and reference artifacts used to map a physical camera view to the peer
canvas it represents.

Record:

- camera, lens, subject canvas, and Individual routing identity;
- intrinsic calibration and lens-distortion parameters;
- canvas corner mapping, crop, orientation, and perspective transform;
- exposure, white-balance, and color-reference procedure;
- acceptable drift and recalibration triggers;
- secure storage and restoration of calibration data.

Acceptance images must prove that the view excludes visitor areas under normal
alignment tolerances.
