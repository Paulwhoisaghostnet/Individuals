# Optics

Lens and working-distance requirements for framing peer canvases from their final
physical positions.

Record:

- sensor format, focal length, aperture, focus range, and compatible mount;
- camera-to-canvas distance and required horizontal and vertical field of view;
- distortion, vignetting, chromatic aberration, flare, and edge sharpness;
- depth-of-field and focus-lock strategy;
- filters, hoods, protective windows, and reflection control;
- adjustment range and a method for locking alignment after calibration.

Intentional optical distortion should be specified separately from unwanted or
unstable variation.
