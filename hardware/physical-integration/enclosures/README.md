# Enclosures

Protective and presentational housings for compute, cameras, power devices, and
other non-canvas hardware.

Document and validate:

- external dimensions, materials, finish, weight, and visual relationship to the work;
- equipment clearances, airflow paths, filters, and acoustic treatment;
- tool and access requirements for routine service;
- ingress, impact, tamper, and visitor-reach risks appropriate to the venue;
- grounding, flammability, cable entry, and edge treatment;
- repeatable assembly and replacement without disturbing calibrated neighbors.
