# Thermal management

Heat generation, airflow, cooling, and acoustic effects for individual enclosures
and shared equipment areas.

Document and validate:

- thermal design power and measured load for every active component;
- inlet conditions, exhaust path, clearance, and recirculation risk;
- component temperature under representative sustained workloads;
- passive cooling, fan curve, filter, and redundancy strategy;
- maximum acceptable sound level at visitor positions;
- alert, throttling, shutdown, cleaning, and filter-replacement thresholds.
