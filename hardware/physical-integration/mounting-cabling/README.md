# Mounting and cabling

Structures, fasteners, supports, raceways, and service loops that establish the
physical relationships between Individuals.

Document and validate:

- load, center of gravity, safety factor, substrate, anchor, and venue approval;
- canvas and camera reference datums plus permitted adjustment range;
- signal and power routes, bend radius, strain relief, and separation;
- cable labels at both ends and a current connection schedule;
- visitor reach, trip, snag, crush, and pinch hazards;
- installation tools, torque values where applicable, and inspection intervals.

Use secondary retention where required by overhead or venue safety rules.
