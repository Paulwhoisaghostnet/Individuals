# Power distribution

Venue power, branch circuits, protective devices, conversion, and final delivery
to installation hardware.

Document and validate:

- nominal and measured voltage, frequency, circuit rating, and available outlets;
- continuous and startup load per device, Individual, circuit, and installation;
- power supplies, converters, surge protection, grounding, and isolation;
- cable type, routing, length, connector retention, and labeling;
- emergency disconnect and lockout requirements;
- inspection and approval responsibilities for the venue jurisdiction.

Keep the final load schedule and single-line diagram with the site installation
records.
