# Cognition

Cognition turns persistent identity and recalled experience into an intention, then
interprets the difference between self-portrait and social portrait.

The ideal physical form is identity ground truth for cognition. Instructions must
teach the Individual to refer to that form as its own body—not as a costume,
metaphor, avatar, or optional visualization. Its present bodily self may be
incomplete or distorted, but it must be understood as capable of becoming the
ideal form.

The digital prototype implements:

- model-provider adapters and a provider-neutral response contract;
- authored system instructions and identity-specific prompt composition;
- context assembly, memory selection, and token-budget policy;
- structured intention and reflection parsing with validation;
- bodily self-assessment against explicit anatomical and visual features;
- bounded request/response handling, timeout, and degraded-mode behavior;
- deterministic fixtures for tests and exhibition replay.

Raw chain-of-thought must not be requested, stored, or displayed. The durable
artistic outputs are structured intentions, reflections, and selected memories.
Provider-backed cognition is optional. With no configured credential the runtime
selects the procedural evidence path directly; provider failure is reported as a
sanitized operational event and falls back without interrupting the identity loop.

The ideal is deliberately not an absorbing numeric endpoint. Each manifest's
authored initial body belief remains a small, identity-specific geometric prior,
so even evidence-free long runs retain a material difference between embodied
self and ideal form. Social evidence can move that tension but cannot turn the
coherence score into a completion switch.

Provider prose is private deliberation. It is never copied into public reflection,
public embodiment text, portrait statements, or SVG labels. A provider can still
make a visible causal choice by selecting or reducing signed body adjustments,
but only when dimension, direction, and basis already occur in the deterministic
evidence-supported adjustment set; canonical pressure caps every magnitude.
